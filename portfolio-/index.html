Machine learning

Machine learning enables algorithms to analyze data, identify patterns, and improve performance on a specific task over time, allowing systems to make predictions or decisions based on new, unseen data.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines, enabling them to perform tasks such as reasoning, learning, problem-solving, and understanding natural language, often through techniques like machine learning and neural networks.

Deep learning

Deep learning is a specialized subset of machine learning that uses neural networks with multiple layers (known as deep neural networks) to model complex patterns in large datasets, enabling advanced tasks such as image recognition, natural language processing, and autonomous decision-making.

Natural language

Natural language processing (NLP) is a field of artificial intelligence that focuses on the interaction between computers and humans through natural language, enabling machines to understand, interpret, and respond to human language in a way that is both meaningful and useful.
